[224 - 9-12-2009]
Support to user's callback functions on crud operations: 

$form->setCallbackAfterInsert('my_function'); OR $form->setCallbackAfterInsert(array('my_class','my_method')); 
The inserted values will be passed as function args 

$form->setCallbackAfterUpdate('my_function'); OR $form->setCallbackAfterUpdate(array('my_class','my_method')); 
The updated values will be passed as function args 

$form->setCallbackAfterDelete('my_function'); OR $form->setCallbackAfterDelete(array('my_class','my_method')); 
The condition where will be passed as function args
